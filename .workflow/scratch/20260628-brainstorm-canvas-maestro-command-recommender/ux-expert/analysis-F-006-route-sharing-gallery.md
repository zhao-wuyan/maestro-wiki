# F-006 Route Sharing and Gallery — UX View

The share flow SHOULD expose two separate controls: `Copy share link` and `Publish to gallery`. Publishing to gallery MUST be opt-in.

The management view MUST show shared route status and allow two distinct actions: `Remove from gallery` and `Revoke link`.

Edge notes SHOULD be editable from the connection itself or a small edge popover so users can explain why a transition was chosen.
