# REQ-006: Route Sharing and Gallery

The product SHOULD support standalone share URLs, optional public gallery publishing, and share management.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Sharing MUST distinguish private share links from public gallery publishing.
- Users MUST be able to remove a shared route from the gallery.
- Users MUST be able to revoke a share link.
- Shared route records MUST include visibility and revocation fields.
- Public gallery implementation MAY be deferred until storage/API exists.
